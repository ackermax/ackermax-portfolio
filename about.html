About

Max Ackerman is an avid full-stack web developer with previous collegiate education in the world of film and theatre.


With a passion for pushing the future of technology and entertainment forward, Max has a mind brimming with creativity along with an analytical side that gives that creativity drive and a solid grounding in reality.


Max is also an excellent writer and editor, having managed and written content for Orlando Informer, one of the largest third-party travel websites, and Tambourine, a full-service marketing agency based in Fort Lauderdale, Florida.


An excellent photographer, Max brings his sense of composition and color to his web designs to create the best product possible for the user.


With his passion, creativity, work ethic, and attitude, Max Ackerman is the perfect addition to make your project or team the absolute best it can be.
